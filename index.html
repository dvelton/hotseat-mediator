Hotseat Mediator

A structured settlement negotiation tool

🔒 Your data stays private. All negotiation data remains in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server.

How It Works

This tool lets two parties explore whether there is a dollar amount at which both would settle — without either side losing bargaining position.

The tool steps through a range of dollar amounts. Each party privately indicates whether they would accept or reject each amount. If both accept the same amount, the tool reveals the match. If one accepts and the other rejects, neither side learns the other's answer.
